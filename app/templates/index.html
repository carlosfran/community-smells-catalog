Definition of Community Smell

The term "Community Smells" describes sets of organizational and social circumstances with implicit causal relationships that, when observed, can indicate the presence of social debt, resulting in additional costs to the project. Due to its significance, several research efforts have been dedicated to the detection and mitigation of Community Smells.
