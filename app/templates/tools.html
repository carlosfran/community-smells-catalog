Tools

Some tools and interesting forms to detect Community Smells.

CodeFace4Smell

Extension of CodeFace tool that detects Organizational silo, Black cloud, Lone wolf, and Radio silence [14].

YOSHI

Identify development communities, diagnose their health based on six factors, and potential for monitoring social debt [22].

CSDetector

It's a tool to automatically detect community smells in their project [5].

Framework DAHLIA

Metrics-based framework: decision popularity, decision awareness, mean architecture incommunicability, and average per-person delay [18].

Socio-technical Quality Framework

40 socio-technical quality metrics comprising five categories: Developer Social network, Sociotechnical, Core Community Members, Turnover, and Social Network Analysis [11, 4, 7].

Social Network Analysis (SNA)

Social-network-based models for building formal definitions of community smells [11] and quantify forms of social debt in software architecture activities [20].

Statiscal model

Prediction models for correlating community smells and code smells [21], predicting bug-prone code components [8], examining the impact of gender diversity in teams on mitigating community smells [16,15], automatically detecting community smells in open-source projects with high precision [10, 9, 5], and predicting community smells on software developers based on sentiment analysis [6, 1].
