Organizational Silo

This community smell is associated with task coordination problems. Software development tasks are sometimes not interconnected with each other. This smell sets challenges to check task dependencies and non-conducive conditions for effective communication among teammates performing tasks. This scenario puts the socio-technical congruence at risk.

Causes

This smell has causes from the following factors of teamwork: Communication, Composition, Coordination, Context, Culture.

  • Geographic dispersion
  • High decoupling between tasks
  • Lack of communication
  • Lack of cooperation in checking task dependencies
  • Low team turnover can lead to a division in consolidated sub-communities

Effects

This smells affects the following factors of teamwork: Cooperation, Community smell, Context, Coordination, Communication, Technical debt effect.

  • Community Smell - Solutions Defiance
  • Tunnel vision with a consequent lack of creativity and lack of cooperation
  • Tunnel vision with a consequent lack of collaboration
  • Developers make architecture decisions on their own without the necessary background and premises
  • Developers make architecture decisions on their own using different format every time
  • Community filled with wasted resources e.g., time
  • Decaying communication across sub-communities and consequent negative effects on developers situational awareness
  • Duplication of code

Refactoring

  • Create communication plan
  • Social wiki
  • Mentoring
  • Restructure the community
  • Cohesion Exercising
  • Monitoring closely how team members communicate and share information.
  • Introduce a social-rewarding mechanism
