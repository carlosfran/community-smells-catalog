Invisible Architecting

This smell describes a situation in which teammates document software architecture decisions and register meeting agreements inconsistently. Consequently, the descriptions of such decisions, necessary for the software architecture process, become invisible. The lack of accurate information generates, for example, decision unawareness and conflicts among teammates.

Causes

This smell has causes from the following factors of teamwork: Composition, Coordination.

  • Architecture decisions are made or changed rapidly
  • Architecture documents are not used properly and/or few architects are present.
  • Architecture decisions are too big to implement and new team is added to the development network to implement the changes

Effects

This smells affects the following factors of teamwork: Context, Technical debt effect, Cognition, Community smell.

  • Decision unawareness
  • Product version and architecture misalignment
  • Community Smell - Solutions Defiance
  • Time waste

Refactoring

  • Architecture board
